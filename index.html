About Coach

CARLO ANCELOTTI

Carlo Ancelotti is an Italian former footballer, the current head coach of Real Madrid and widely regarded as one of the most successful managers in the world. Ancelotti is known for flexibility in his formations, having opted to accommodate star players wherever possible despite a personal preference for a 4-4-2 formation.
